Foreground Privacy Policy

Foreground is an open-source application that aims to protect user privacy. This privacy policy
discloses how your data is stored and used, and may be updated from time to time as necessary.

Foreground does not collect any user information other than what you enter into your task list. This
data is stored locally on your device. Your task data is not viewable singularly or in aggregate by
the developer nor shared with third parties not described in this privacy policy.

You the user may optionally decide to enable sync. In this case, user data will be sent securely to
the taskserver you specify. Data located on these servers is subject to the privacy policies of the
server operator.
